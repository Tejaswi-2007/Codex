About Codex Club

Codex Club is where creativity meets code. Built by passionate learners and guided by expert mentors, we focus on hands-on learning that turns ideas into reality. Established under the guidance of faculty mentors, the club provides a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and apply their knowledge through real-world projects. Through workshops, coding challenges, and project-based learning, Codex encourages analytical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills essential for academic and professional success. The club aims to bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry requirements, reparing students to excel in today’s technology-driven world.
