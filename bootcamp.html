Ethical Hacking Sessions to Students

A student-focused ethical hacking session introduces participants to the fundamentals of cybersecurity, designed to show how real-world attacks work and how to protect digital systems. It helps students understand how cyberattacks happen, how to defend systems, and how to build a career in cybersecurity.
