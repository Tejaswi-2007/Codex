About Smart India Hackathon 2025

Smart India Hackathon (SIH) is a national-level innovation initiative organized by the Ministry of Education (MoE) and AICTE. It provides students with a platform to solve real-world problems through creativity, technology, and teamwork. Since its inception in 2017, SIH has become one of India’s largest innovation movements, empowering young minds to think beyond textbooks and develop impactful solutions for society.

The 2025 edition continues this vision by offering problem statements from ministries, industries, PSUs, NGOs, and state departments across multiple domains such as agriculture, cybersecurity, health tech, automation, rural development, sustainability, travel & tourism, and smart education. Students gain hands-on exposure, industry insights, and opportunities to work on practical challenges that contribute toward nation-building.

Institute-Level Hackathon at IMRD Shirpur

R. C. Patel Educational Trust’s IMRD Shirpur conducted the institute-level round of SIH 2025, following the guidelines of the Ministry of Education’s Innovation Cell (MoE–AICTE). The event was inaugurated by Dr. Vaishali Patil (Director, IMRD), who appreciated the efforts of the organizing team and encouraged students to keep innovating.

The entire coordination and management of the hackathon were handled by Codex Club, ensuring smooth execution, proper guidance, and high-quality participation. Students presented their innovative ideas and demonstrated the initial concepts of their projects with dedication and enthusiasm.

Outcome

The institute-level SIH helped students strengthen their innovation mindset, problem-solving skills, and teamwork abilities. After evaluation by academic and technical experts, top-performing teams were shortlisted for further guidance and preparation toward the national SIH 2025. The event successfully encouraged creativity, leadership, and real-world application of knowledge among students.
