Your Dream View Session

An inspiring initiative organised under Codex Club

“Your Dream View Session” was organised to help students gain clarity about their future goals, understand modern career possibilities, and explore the right skill paths to achieve them. Our college actively participated, and the entire management & coordination was handled by **Codex Club**.

About the Session

The session focused on empowering students through awareness, exposure, and practical guidance. It encouraged students to think beyond traditional career views and understand real-world industry expectations.

Key Highlights

  • Introduction to top emerging career paths
  • Guidance on skill development and industry tools
  • Motivation towards self-learning and innovation
  • Clarity on how to choose the right future direction
  • Interactive discussion with practical insights

Outcome

Students gained a clearer understanding of their aspirations and learned how to build a practical roadmap for their future careers. The session successfully created enthusiasm and inspired students to take active steps towards learning and personal growth.

Managed & Organised by Codex Club – Empowering Students Toward Innovation
